FASHIONABLY LATE $ 24

Ingredients

Suntory Toki

Yamamomo juice

2 dashes of Sakura bitters

Instructions

Mixing Style: Stirred

Served in: Frozen rocks glass with King Cube

Garnished with a Yamamomo Berry

Do NOT FORGET: To be served with a soy ramekin for the Yamamomo pit

Flavour Note

Spirit Forward, Balanced

Alcohol Amount: 2oz

Knowledge Base

Toki means "time" in Japanese. Suntory Whisky Toki is a silky Japanese whisky with a subtle sweet-and-spicy finish.

Sakura Bitters Tasting Notes: Cherry blossom, yellow cherry flesh, anise, salinic, rich, cardamom, cinnamon, cedar aroma, subtly floral, very mild bitterness.

MIDORI GARDEN $ 23

Ingredients

Tanqueray Gin

McGuinness Melon

Tio Pepe Sherry

El Gobernador Pisco

Lemon Juice

Thai Bird Simple Syrup

Instructions

Mixing Style: Shaken

Served in: 1920s Retro Fizz Coupe

Garnished with mandolin star fruit & flower petal

Flavour Note

Tart, Spicy, Sharp flavor, Melon dominant

Alcohol Amount: 2.5oz

Knowledge Base

El Gobernador Pisco: Chilean pisco from famed wine producer Miguel Torres. Made with Moscatel grapes, which provide aromatic floral notes, as well as notes of citrus, this has a creamy texture and a long finish.

Tio Pepe Sherry: Tio Pepe is a Fino style, the most delicate and elegante of the sherry styles, aged for four years under flor, a unique layer of yeast produced naturally in Jerez.

Thai Bird Syrup is made with Simple Syrup (Equal part water mixed with equal amount of sugar) and Bird's eye chili with spicyness of about 50,000 to 100,000 Scoville units
